Who are We?

Yellowstone Equity is an unbiased and independent research advisory group with a focus on Indian equities. We provide 8 to 15 high conviction investment ideas, on average, with a typical horizon of 3 to 5 years to help you compound your wealth.

Our founder, Ankit Agrawal, is an experienced investment analyst with 8+ years of research experience across asset classes and geographies. Prior to Yellowstone, Ankit was a core member of the absolute return investment team at Investcorp in New York that was managing $1bn+ in discretionary client assets at the time.

Our Name

Our name is inspired by Yellowstone National Park, one of the oldest and most visited national parks in US. Our nature-loving founder used this name in remembrance of his last trip in US before moving to India. Yellowstone National Park is known for its hot springs. To us, Yellowstone National Park embodies entrepreneurship with calm, beautiful, peaceful and embracing qualities on the surface, bolstered by enormous amount of fire, energy and passion at the core.

Our logo

Our logo houses letter "Y" within a box. Just like the letter "Y" is made up of three divergent lines converging to a single point, our research process entails diverging across different perspectives to converge to a pointed recommendation. "Y" as a shorthand for "Why?" also highlights our "Question Everything" spirit. The box housing "Y" signifies that all our activities are bound by a robust risk management framework.
